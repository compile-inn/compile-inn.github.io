  • Hearsay generator

    • Listen to a hearsay from the Compile inn's wizard!
    The first program I wrote was a console-based password generator. Designed I bit like an adventure game (very loosely), You would enter rooms in a keep called the Dragon Arm. The dragon would give you a spell - meaning secure passwords based on your settings, The wizard would give you a hearsay - i.e. a passphrase composed of several random words picked from a corpus, which was based on King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table. It takes a corpus and returns a string of randomly selected words. More often than not, the result is pretty humourous. I do not remember where I got the ASCII art from, but it is very cute.
